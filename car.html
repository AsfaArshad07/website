We offer the best Car Insurance Policies, Coverage and Protection so that you can always reach your destination! Ensure the absolute safety of your automobile from the 3Ts: Theft, Third Part and Total Loss only with the best Motor Insurance Policies by Asreela



cover



    I. Total Loss or Constructive Total Loss of vehicle:


    a. Accidental external means
    b. Fire, external explosion, self-ignition, lightning and frost
    c. Riot, strike, malicious act, civil commotion and terrorism
    d. Flood, hail, wind, hurricane, cyclone, tornado and typhoon damages
    e. Earthquake, volcanic eruption or other convulsion of nature


* Constructive total loss is defined in the policy as when the actual cost of repairs exceeds 60% of the market value or the Insurance estimated value of the vehicle— whichever is lower




    II. Loss of vehicle in case of Theft, burglary or snatching provided that:


    a.The vehicle is used only for private and domestic use
    b. The Insurance vehicle is older than 5 years at the time of insurance from the manufacturing year
    c. Basis of indemnity be the market value or the Insurance estimated value, whichever is lessm


    III. Total Loss or Constructive Total Loss of vehicle:


    a.Death or bodily injury to a third party (except for the person employed by the Insurance)
    b. Damage to property of a third party (except for the property held in trust or custody by the insured of a third party)
    c. Indemnity to any person driving any on Insurance order/permission given that he is not entitled to indemnity under any other policy
    d.Benefit to the Insurance even when he is driving a vehicle not belonging to him and not hired by him under a hire purchase agreements
    e.In case of death of a third party, the company will indemnify the deceased’s personal representatives in the terms of and subject to the limitations of the policy


conditions



    a.An immediate notice to the company to be Insurance in case of a claim arising
    b. In case of theft of the Insurance vehicle, an immediate notice to the police is mandatory
    c. In case the Insurance loses his original excise duty and taxation papers, he should immediately inform the company within 48 hours
    d. Cancellation of policy by the company on a 7 days’ notice with return of premium on pro rata basis
